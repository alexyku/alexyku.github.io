About Me

My name is Alexander Ku, and I am a software engineering intern on the Google Brain team. Broadly speaking, I am interested in applied machine learning and statistics for the biological and cognitive sciences. I collaborate closely with Ryan Poplin, Ashish Vaswani, and Pi-Chuan Chang on the Google Brain team; Joey Gonzalez and Nir Yosef at UC Berkeley; and Tom Griffiths at Princeton University.

Interests: Deep Learning, Generative Modeling, Statistical Bioinformatics, Computational Biology, Computational Cognitive Science
