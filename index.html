I'm a PhD student in the Computational Cognitive Science Lab at Princeton University and a research scientist at Google DeepMind.

My research interests broadly falls under the umbrella of cognitive science, computational neuroscience, and machine learning. In particular, I'm interested in quantifying the degree to which language contributes to nonverbal intelligence (e.g., visual reasoning, symbolic logic, planning) in both people and AI systems.

Previously, I've worked on image generation, grounded language learning, and computational genomics.
