Alexander Ku

I'm a software engineer at Google Research where I work on grounded language learning.

Lately, I've been interested in concept learning. That is, how concepts form from perceptual experiences, language, and introspection. My current research uses tools from interpretable machine learning to establish computational parallels between machine learning and psychological theory.
