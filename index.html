About Me

My name is Alexander Ku, and I am a research software engineer in Google AI working on grounded language learning in interactive virtual environments. Broadly speaking, I am interested agents that learn by interacting with their surroundings and each other using language. While these ideas are not new to developmental psychologists and linguists, implementing them computationally requires continued advancements in NLP, computer vision, and robotics.
