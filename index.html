Hi! I'm a software engineer at Google Research. Previously, I completed my bachelors and masters at UC Berkeley where I was affiliated with the Computational Cognitive Science Lab.

My research focuses on establishing shared computational principles between learning in people and deep neural networks. In particular, how the mechanisms that enable generalization in both result in potentially harmful associations and biases. This broadly falls under the umbrella of cognitive science and interpretability.
